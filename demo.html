How to use

  1. Click the ○ button in the toolbar to activate annotation mode
  2. Hover over elements to see them highlighted
  3. Click on any element to add an annotation
  4. Press Cmd+Shift+F (Mac) or Ctrl+Shift+F (Windows) to toggle
  5. Press C to copy annotations to clipboard
